the bygone memory of you
never bid me adieu
it stirs from time to time
  in the recesses of my mind

the bygone you
still wears that green cardigan
still grows green onions and somehow 
loves me still

everyone tells me that the you i knew
the you in my dreams
the you by my side
is bygone.

and maybe i want it to be

oh but i can’t forget
so how can it really be
bygone ? how can it be
gone, begone, begone!
